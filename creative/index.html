Rhythm is the framework that holds everything together. Before architecture, before strategy — the drum kit. Precision, timing, and the discipline to hold a structure while everything around you moves.

Record digging brought something different: patience, specificity, the hunt for something nobody else is playing. You don't find the right record by browsing — you find it by showing up to the crate every time. That persistence migrates everywhere.
