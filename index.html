About
The Senate isn't a metaphor. It's how I actually run.

I'm Grant — a Solution Architect who bleeds Detroit sports, treats music like medicine, and hunts vintage sportswear like it's a second career. I also collect Star Wars Lego, run Counter-Strike like it's a job interview, and can't stop thinking about what AI is about to break next. I'm of Nordic heritage and proud of it — the mythology was never far away growing up, and eventually it earned a permanent seat. The Senate is what happens when you stop apologizing for the range.

Professionally, I specialize in critical services — healthcare systems where the stakes are real and complexity doesn't forgive shortcuts. I work client-facing and in the weeds. I push back when something doesn't add up, and make it happen when it does.
